Fall Festival 2026

Mark your calendars — the Griffin community's most beloved tradition is coming back bigger and better than ever.

4th Annual

Valor Leander Fall Festival

🎃   Fall 2026   🎃

More Information Coming Soon

The Fall Festival is the biggest event of the Valor Leander school year — a beloved tradition that brings our entire Griffin community together for an unforgettable day of fun, fellowship, and school spirit. It takes an incredible amount of volunteers and family support to make it happen, and every pair of hands makes a difference.

We are so excited to begin planning the 4th Annual Fall Festival starting Summer 2026. If you'd like to help plan and execute this year's festival, please fill out the PSO Volunteer Interest Form — we'd love to have you on the team!
