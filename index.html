Valor Leander PSO

A Culture of Contribution

Our Mission

To help foster friendship and build community among Valor families through service to our faculty, school, and scholars.

Valor's PSO exists to support and enrich the school community through purposeful service, meaningful partnership, and active engagement. We strive to uphold the school’s values by fostering a spirit of unity among parents, staff, and students by working together to serve with excellence, humility, and joy. Rooted in the belief that parents play a vital role in the formation of each child, we are committed to:

Parent Community

Encourage a strong, supportive parent community aligned with Valor's mission.

Faculty Support

Assist and uplift faculty through acts of service and heartfelt appreciation.

Student Flourishing

Promote student growth by volunteering time, talents, and resources.

School Partnership

Partner with school leadership to maintain a culture of virtue, learning, and love.

Through our shared efforts, we seek to reflect the heart of Valor Leander — building a school culture where families are connected, service is joyful, and every student is surrounded by care and encouragement.
