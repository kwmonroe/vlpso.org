Welcome!

Teacher Appreciation Week is coming up May 5-9, and we're helping our incredible staff take a "first-class trip around the world" -- without ever leaving school.

To ensure every faculty and staff member feels celebrated, each class has been assigned 3-4 individuals to recognize with a small gift on Tuesday and a note of gratitude on Thursday. Of course, you're welcome to recognize others as well.

Here's how you can contribute:

  • Make a monetary donation or volunteer to help the Coffee Bar (Monday), Snack Lounge (Wednesday), and Teacher Luncheon (Friday)
  • Bring a small gift from our faculty/staff wish lists on Tuesday
  • Write a short note of appreciation with your child for delivery on Thursday

Teacher Appreciation Week Plan:

  • Monday: "First-Class", Coffee Bar in the Faculty Commons
  • Tuesday: "Baggage Claim", Parents drop off wish list gifts
  • Wednesday: "Business Lounge", Snack Station
  • Thursday: "Postcards of Gratitude", Parents and children write thank-you notes
  • Friday: "Around the World", Teacher Luncheon

Thank You!

Thank you for helping us celebrate the amazing people who support our students every day. Your involvement makes all the difference.

Our dedicated teachers strive to cultivate wisdom and virtue in their students through the timeless principles of classical education. They often need specific resources to enrich their classrooms and bring history, literature, logic, and the arts to life.

This site provides a central place to find and support the classroom needs of our K-12 educators. Browse the wish lists and consider contributing to the formation of the next generation.
