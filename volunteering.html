Volunteer at Valor Leander

Volunteering is one of the most meaningful ways you can support our school community. Whether you have an hour a month or a few hours a week, there is a place for you here. Every act of service — big or small — makes a difference for our students and faculty.

Ways to Get Involved

  • Teacher Appreciation: Help coordinate meals, gifts, and events that celebrate our faculty and staff throughout the year.
  • Classroom Support: Assist teachers with special projects, read-alouds, or classroom events.
  • School Events: Lend a hand at school-wide events like orientations, celebrations, and fundraisers.
  • Wish Lists: Purchase items directly from a teacher's wish list to support their classroom needs.
  • Behind the Scenes: Help with planning, communications, or other PSO organizational tasks — no campus visit required.
