Volunteer at Valor Leander

Volunteering is one of the most meaningful ways you can support our school community. Whether you have an hour a month or a few hours a week, there is a place for you here. Every act of service — big or small — makes a difference for our students and faculty.

Ways to Get Involved

Faculty Appreciation

Help coordinate meals, gifts, and events that celebrate our faculty and staff throughout the year.

Classroom Support

Assist teachers with special projects or classroom events throughout the school year.

School Events

Lend a hand at school-wide events including orientations, celebrations, and fundraisers.

Wish Lists

Purchase items directly from a teacher's wish list to support their classroom needs and goals.

Behind the Scenes

Support planning, communications, or PSO organizational tasks — no campus visit required.
