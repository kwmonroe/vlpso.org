Our Purpose

The Valor Leander Parent Service Organization (PSO) is a volunteer-led group dedicated to supporting the students, faculty, and staff of Valor Leander. We work in partnership with the school administration and families to enhance the educational experience and foster a strong, vibrant school community, grounded in the principles of classical education.

What We Do

Our efforts are focused on several key areas:

🏆 Supporting Faculty & Staff

We organize teacher appreciation events, coordinate classroom volunteers, and help fulfill classroom needs to ensure our educators feel valued and well-resourced.

📚 Enhancing Student Life

The PSO helps organize and fund activities and events that enrich the student experience beyond the core curriculum.

🤝 Building Community

We create opportunities for parents and families to connect through social events, informational meetings, and volunteer initiatives.

🎁 Fundraising

We raise funds to support specific school projects, provide resources for classrooms, and contribute to the overall mission of Valor Leander.

PSO Handbook

New to the PSO or want a deeper look at how we operate? Our PSO Handbook covers everything you need to know about our organization, policies, and how to get involved.

Get Involved!

Every Valor Leander parent is automatically a member of the PSO! We encourage you to get involved in any way you can, whether it's volunteering your time, participating in events, or contributing to fundraising efforts. Your participation directly benefits our students and strengthens our unique school culture.

Together, we help Valor Leander fulfill its mission of cultivating wisdom, virtue, and a lifelong love of learning.

PSO Monthly Meetings

Monthly PSO Meetings are virtual and all families are invited to join in on the conversation to be more involved. Meetings are held at 10:00 AM, normally the third Thursday of each month, unless otherwise specified in the PSO Newsletter.
