About the PSO

Our Purpose

To provide parents and guardians with a variety of ways to serve and support the Valor Leander school community (students, faculty, and leadership) throughout the year.

What We Do

Our efforts are focused on three key areas of service:

Supporting Faculty & Staff

We organize teacher appreciation events, coordinate classroom volunteers, and help fulfill classroom needs so our educators feel valued and well-resourced.

Enhancing Student Life

The PSO helps organize and support activities and events that enrich the student experience and complement Valor's classical curriculum.

Building Community

We create meaningful opportunities for parents and families to connect through social events, informational meetings, and volunteer initiatives.

Get Involved

Every Valor Leander parent is automatically a member of the PSO. We encourage you to get involved in any way that fits your schedule and interests — whether that is volunteering your time, attending events, or contributing to fundraising efforts. Your participation directly benefits our students and strengthens our unique school culture.

Before volunteering or joining an active PSO role, please review our volunteering page to understand the requirements and current opportunities available.
